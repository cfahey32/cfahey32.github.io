Welcome To My Final Project Page!

This is my Rock, Paper, Scissors program. Hit the start button to begin the game. My program lets players choose to play either rock, paper, or scissors by selecting one of the corresponding buttons. Once the player makes a choice the computer then randomly selects its choice. The program then chooses the winner and the scores are updated accordingly. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. Good luck.
